## Common questions

**Is this a real aura reading?**
No. Auracard is a fun, playful experience using AI to generate a vibe-style "aura" based on a photo. It is not a spiritual or medical service.

**Where does my photo go?**
Each photo is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a reading, then stored locally on your device. We don't keep copies. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy.md).

**Why is my Deep Aura locked?**
Deep Aura is part of Pro ($1.99/month). Pro also removes the ad placeholder and removes the daily limit.

**How do I delete a reading?**
Long-press a reading in the History grid → Delete.

**Cancel Pro:**
iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Auracard → Cancel.
