# Support — Drafted

For bug reports, feature requests, or any questions, email **divinejdavis@gmail.com**. We aim to respond within 48 hours.

## Common questions

**Why did my draft not generate?**
Drafted needs an internet connection to call the Claude API. If the request fails, tap "Re-roll" to try again.

**How do I subscribe to Pro?**
Tap "Upgrade" in Settings, or tap any locked feature. Pro is $4.99/month, auto-renewing, managed through your Apple ID. You can cancel anytime in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

**Can I delete my saved drafts?**
Yes — swipe-to-delete on any item in the History tab.

**Does Drafted send my texts anywhere?**
The text of your situation is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate drafts. Saved drafts live only on your device. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy.md) for details.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Drafted → Cancel.
