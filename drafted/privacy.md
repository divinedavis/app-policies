# Privacy Policy — Drafted

**Effective:** May 4, 2026

## What we collect

- The text you type into the app (situations, context, generated drafts).
- Your app subscription status, managed by Apple.

We do not collect names, email addresses, contact lists, location, or device identifiers.

## How we use it

- The text you submit is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate draft variations.
- Drafts you save are stored on your device only, in a local SwiftData store.
- Your subscription status is managed by Apple's StoreKit and used to gate the daily draft limit.

## Third-party services

- **Anthropic (Claude API)** — receives the text of your situation and chosen tone in order to generate drafts. Anthropic does not use API inputs to train models. See https://www.anthropic.com/privacy.
- **Apple StoreKit** — manages subscription status. See https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

We do not share your data with any other third party. We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or trackers.

## Storage

Your drafts and history live in a local SwiftData store on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers (we do not run any servers).

## Sign in with Apple

If you choose to sign in with Apple, Apple gives us a stable identifier (and optionally your email). We use it only to associate your subscription status with your account. You can revoke this access at any time in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Sign-In & Security → Sign in with Apple.

## Children

Drafted is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

If our practices change, we will update this page and the "Effective" date above.
