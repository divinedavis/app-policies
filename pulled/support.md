## Common questions

**Why can I only pull one card per day?**
That's the design. Daily ritual, daily reflection. The button unlocks again at midnight local time.

**My widget isn't updating.**
After your daily pull, the widget refreshes automatically. If it doesn't, remove and re-add the widget to your lock screen.

**What's in Pro?**
Pro ($0.99/month) removes the ad placeholder and unlocks three additional widget designs.

**Are these real tarot interpretations?**
Pulled uses an AI (Anthropic's Claude) to generate one playful interpretation per card. It's for entertainment and reflection, not divination advice.

**Cancel Pro:**
iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Pulled → Cancel.
