# Privacy Policy — Pulled

**Effective:** May 4, 2026

## What we collect

- The tarot cards you pull each day, with the AI-generated interpretations and timestamps.
- Your app subscription status, managed by Apple.

We do not collect names, email addresses, contact lists, location, or device identifiers.

## How we use it

- Each daily pull and interpretation is stored on your device only.
- The card name (e.g., "The Fool") is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a short interpretation. Your personal information is never included.
- Pulled also writes the current card to a shared App Group container so the lock-screen widget can read it.

## Third-party services

- **Anthropic (Claude API)** — receives only the card name to generate an interpretation. See https://www.anthropic.com/privacy.
- **Apple StoreKit** — manages subscription status.

We do not share data with any other third party. No analytics, no advertising SDKs.

## Storage

Past pulls live in a local SwiftData store on your device. The widget reads from a local App Group container. Nothing is uploaded.

## Children

Pulled is not directed at children under 13.

## Changes

If our practices change, we will update this page.
