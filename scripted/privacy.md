# Privacy Policy — Scripted

**Effective:** May 4, 2026

## What we collect

- The journal entries you write (text content, category, date).
- Optional prompt-suggestion requests sent to the Claude API.
- Your app subscription status, managed by Apple.

We do not collect names, email addresses, contact lists, location, or device identifiers.

## How we use it

- Your journal entries are stored on your device only, in a local SwiftData store. They are never uploaded.
- When you tap "✨ Suggest prompt," a short, generic prompt-generation request is sent to Anthropic's Claude API. Your entry text is **not** sent.
- Subscription status gates the monthly entry limit and theme unlocks.

## Third-party services

- **Anthropic (Claude API)** — used only to generate generic writing prompts on demand. See https://www.anthropic.com/privacy.
- **Apple StoreKit** — manages subscription status.

We do not share your data with any other third party. No analytics, ads, or trackers.

## Storage

Your journal entries live in a local SwiftData store on your device only. They are not uploaded.

## Sign in with Apple

Optional. If used, Apple provides a stable identifier and (optionally) your email. We use this only to associate subscription status across devices.

## Children

Scripted is not directed at children under 13.

## Changes

If our practices change, we will update this page and the "Effective" date.
