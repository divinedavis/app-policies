## Common questions

**What is "scripting"?**
Scripting is writing about your life in the present tense as if your goals have already happened. Scripted gives you three categories: Script your future, Letter to future self, Daily intentions.

**How do I unlock more themes?**
Pro ($2.99/month) unlocks Lavender, Forest, and Midnight in addition to the free Cream theme.

**Why am I limited to 5 entries per month?**
Free tier limit. Pro unlocks unlimited entries.

**Can I export my entries?**
Not yet — we're working on it. Email us if this is important to you so we can prioritize it.

**How do I cancel Pro?**
iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Scripted → Cancel.
