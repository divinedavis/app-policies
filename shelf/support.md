## Common questions

**The barcode scanner isn't recognizing a book.**
Make sure the EAN-13 barcode (on the back of most books) is well-lit and in focus. If a book doesn't have a barcode, use Search by title instead.

**A book's cover doesn't show up.**
Open Library doesn't have covers for every ISBN. The book is still saved — only the cover image is missing. You can re-search by title to try a different edition.

**What's in Pro?**
Pro ($1.99/month) unlocks premium shelf themes (Forest, Library, Linen) and aesthetic widget designs.

**Can I export my shelf?**
Not yet. Email us if this is important to you.

**Cancel Pro:**
iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Shelf → Cancel.
